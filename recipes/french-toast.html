French Toast recipe

Wholesome Hearty French Toast

French Toast is as classic and French as it is Hearty. Be carefull though: eat too much of this fine French cuisine and all that awaits you is crippling obesity. Sweet, hearty, fun for the whole family, but maybe not as french as I had originally advertised.

  • Bread
  • eggs
  • cinnamon
  • milk
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, sugar, cinnamon, and milk together in a sturdy bowl
  2. coat bread in egg concoction
  3. put butter in pan and heat to medium
  4. Cook coated bread until thourly hearty
  5. Enjoy your hearty, authentic meal
