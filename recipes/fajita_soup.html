Fajita Soup

Hearty fajita soup

Fajita Soup is what you get when you combine soup and fajita. It's got heart for days. It may not be the best for your health but what it lacks in health it makes for in tastiness. No one could call it authentic, but no one could call it bad either.

  • 2 table spoons vegetable oil
  • 1 lb chicken
  • 1 ounce fajita seasoning
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 can seasoned black beans

Steps

  1. Heat up vegetable oil in big pan
  2. Add chicken and cook thoroughly
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Add pepper and beans
  5. Heartily enjoy your meal
