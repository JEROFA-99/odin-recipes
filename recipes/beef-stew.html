Beef Stew

Classic Hearty Beef Stew

If you're looking for a meal that is simple, filling, hearty, and delicious to top it all off, then look no further. Beef stew is an excellent choice for lunch, dinner, or even breakfast. This beef stew recipe is so simple a horse could make it, even without hands.

  • 2 pounds of beef(trimmed and cubed)
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 3/4 cup peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 carrots(cut into pieces)

Steps

  1. Heat up broth in a big pot
  2. add meat and cook thoroughly
  3. add carrots and cook till suculent
  4. add salt till it has taste
  5. add peas and call it a diet
  6. enjoy your heard earned slop
